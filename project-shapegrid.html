What It Does

A real-time 3D grid where dark, opaque shapes sit on top of a glowing backplane. Light bleeds through the gaps between shapes, intensifying near your cursor. Built with instanced rendering — thousands of shapes in a single draw call. The illumination lives behind the geometry, creating a backlit silhouette effect.

"Is this just a fancy nightlight?" — Yes. But it's a fancy nightlight with bloom, metalness, and clearcoat. That makes it art.

Features

🔷 Shape Switcher

Switch between Hexagon, Square, Triangle, Circle, Octagon, and Diamond. The grid rebuilds automatically with the correct layout for each shape.

💡 Backlighting

Light glows from behind the shapes, not on them. Move your cursor to reveal the illumination beneath. The shapes stay dark; the light bleeds through the gaps.

✨ Bloom Post-Processing

UnrealBloomPass adds neon glow to the backplane. The light intensifies near the cursor and fades at the edges. Adjustable bloom strength.

🎨 Full Customization

Control grid size, shape size, gap, camera position, colors, materials, animation speed — all from the hidden dat.gui panel.

⚡ Instanced Rendering

Thousands of shapes rendered in a single draw call using THREE.InstancedMesh. 60fps even on modest hardware.

🌈 Auto-Rainbow

Toggle auto-rainbow mode and watch the backlight colors cycle through the spectrum. Because sometimes you just want your grid to vibe.
