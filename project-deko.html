What It Does

AR Deko is a web-based AR room redecorator. Point your phone at a flat surface, tap to place furniture, drag to move, rotate and scale. Browse a catalog of 20+ 3D models across categories (seating, tables, storage, lighting, decor). Measure distances in AR. Save and load room layouts. Upload custom 3D models. Change colors and materials. It's a full interior design toolkit that runs in your browser.

"Why not just use IKEA's AR app?" — Because IKEA's app only shows IKEA furniture. This shows whatever you want. Also, we don't try to sell you a meatball at the end. Though that could be a future feature.

Features

🎯 AR Furniture Placement

Tap detected surfaces to place furniture. Real-world scale with accurate measurements. Support for multiple categories — sofas, chairs, tables, lamps, plants. Your floor is the canvas.

🔄 Object Manipulation

Drag to move. Pinch to rotate. Two-finger tap to scale. Change colors and materials on the fly. It's like playing The Sims, except the furniture is actually in your room.

📐 Measurement Tools

Place measurement points, calculate distances between points, support for metric and imperial. Because "will this couch fit?" is a question you should answer before buying, not after.

💾 Save & Load

Save room layouts locally. Load previously saved arrangements. Export/import as JSON. Because you don't want to re-place 15 items every time you open the app.

📤 Custom Model Upload

Upload your own 3D models. Record video of real objects and generate 3D models using cloud photogrammetry. Because the 20 built-in models might not include that weird lamp your aunt gave you.

📲 PWA Installable

Add to home screen on any platform. No app store needed. No review process. No waiting 3 days for Apple to approve your furniture app. Just tap, install, done.
