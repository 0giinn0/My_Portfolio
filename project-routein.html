What It Does

Rou Tein is a cross-platform habit tracker (React Native for mobile, Next.js for web) that turns self-improvement into a game. You earn XP for completing tasks, coins for daily challenges, badges for streaks, and unlockable color themes. There's a spinning 3D sphere on the homepage that reacts to your touch and changes color based on your progress. It also tracks your meals, shows the weather, has a 7-day workout plan, a Pomodoro timer, and a daily quiz.

"Is this just a Tamagotchi for adults?" — Yes. And I'm not even sorry about it. At least this Tamagotchi helps you do push-ups instead of dying because you forgot to feed it.

Features

🔥 Dopamine Dispenser (Streaks)

Current streak, longest streak, level, XP, coins. It's basically a Tamagotchi for your self-worth. Missing a day costs 100 coins for streak freeze. Choose wisely.

🏆 Daily Challenges

Four things to do every day: complete 3 tasks, log a meal, check the weather, finish the daily quiz. Complete them all and you get a Perfect Day badge. You will become insufferable about this.

🧠 Daily Quiz

5 questions. Different every day. Scored. You don't have to be smart — but it helps. The quiz is the main XP driver, so your brain is literally your currency.

🟣 The Spinny Orb

A Three.js sphere on the homepage that reacts to your touch and changes color based on your progress. It serves no practical purpose. I will not be taking questions.

🍎 Nutrition Tracking

Log food by meal type. Track calories, protein, carbs, fat. The macro rings will guilt you into eating a vegetable. It's passive-aggressive wellness.

🌤️ Weather

7-day forecast, temperature wave chart, seasonal background effects. In winter it snows on the screen. In summer the waves pulse. It's completely unnecessary. You'll love it.

💪 Workouts

Pre-built 7-day calisthenics program + custom workout builder with 100+ exercises. You can finally stop watching fitness reels and actually do a Bulgarian split squat.

⏱️ Pomodoro Timer

25-minute focus sessions with a satisfying animated ring. Completing sessions earns XP. Your ADHD just met its match. The timer doesn't judge — it just watches you stare at the ceiling for 25 minutes.

🎨 Gamification

Badges, unlockable color themes, streak freeze items. It's a video game where the boss fight is your crippling procrastination. The themes cost coins — capitalism, but make it aesthetic.
