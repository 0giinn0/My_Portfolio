About the Art

This collection represents years of character exploration — from early ink studies to refined portraits. Each piece begins as a quick gesture sketch, evolving through multiple iterations until the character's personality shines through.

The samurai series explores the intersection of traditional Japanese aesthetics with contemporary character design. The mobster collection plays with noir aesthetics and bold linework. The portrait studies are exercises in capturing emotion with minimal strokes.
