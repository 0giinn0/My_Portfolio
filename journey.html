The Journey

An engineer's path

From Pakistan to Abu Dhabi, Dubai to Germany — a journey across cultures and continents that shaped who I am today.

Pakistan — Roots & Heritage
Originally from Pakistan, my roots run deep in a culture rich with tradition, family values, and a passion for learning. The Pakistani spirit of resilience and adaptability has shaped who I am today. Growing up between cultures gave me a unique perspective — the ability to navigate different worlds and find common ground.
Pakistan taught me that identity isn't about where you are — it's about where you come from and the values you carry with you.

Abu Dhabi — Where It Began
Born in Abu Dhabi, I experienced the incredible diversity of the UAE from the very start. Growing up in a city where East meets West, where tradition coexists with cutting-edge innovation, shaped my worldview. The cosmopolitan environment taught me to appreciate different perspectives and cultures.
Abu Dhabi is a city of ambition — where the impossible becomes possible, and where I first dreamed of building something meaningful.

Dubai — Design & Discovery
Dubai was where I found my passion for design and technology. At Arab Unity School and later in Design and Technology programs, I discovered the power of visual communication and creative problem-solving. The fast-paced, innovation-driven environment of Dubai pushed me to constantly learn and evolve.
Dubai taught me that speed and quality aren't mutually exclusive — you can move fast and still deliver excellence.
🏫 Arab Unity SchoolDesign & TechPre-Engineering
Germany — The AI Journey
Now based in Bonn, Germany, I'm pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence at IUBH. Germany's tradition of engineering excellence and systematic thinking aligns perfectly with my goals. From freelance design work to English tutoring, every experience has led me here — to the intersection of AI and human-centered design.
Germany is where I'm transforming from a designer into an AI engineer — building systems that don't just look good, but think and learn.
🇩🇪 IUBHAI BachelorBonnEngineering
Luxembourg — Crossroads of Europe
Luxembourg sits at the heart of Europe — a small country with outsized influence. Visiting Luxembourg revealed a world where multilingualism is the norm, where finance meets technology, and where European identity is most tangible.
Luxembourg taught me that the smallest places can have the biggest ideas — it's where cultures converge and innovation thrives.
🇱🇺 EuropeCross-CulturalInnovation
Netherlands — Design & Vision
The Netherlands has always been a beacon for design thinking and engineering precision. From the iconic Dutch design tradition to the thriving tech ecosystem of Amsterdam, visiting the Netherlands reinforced my passion for human-centered technology.
The Netherlands showed me that great design isn't about complexity — it's about clarity, purpose, and the courage to simplify.
🇳🇱 DesignEngineeringAmsterdamInnovation
Oman — Heritage & Horizon
Oman is where tradition meets ambition. From the ancient souks of Muscat to the futuristic vision of Oman Vision 2040, the country embodies a respect for heritage while racing toward innovation. The warmth of Omani hospitality left a lasting impression.
Oman reminded me that progress doesn't mean forgetting where you came from — it means carrying your roots forward.
🇴🇲 HeritageVision 2040MuscatHospitality
Qatar — Ambition in the Desert
Qatar is a nation building the future at breathtaking speed. From the museums of Doha to the innovations at Education City, Qatar shows what happens when vision meets resources. It's a place that pushes you to think bigger.
Qatar taught me that the desert isn't empty — it's full of possibility, waiting for the right idea to take root.
