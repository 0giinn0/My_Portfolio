Klondike Solitaire

← Arcade
How to Play
Goal: Move all cards to the 4 foundation piles (top right), sorted by suit A→K.
Tableau: Build descending, alternating colors (red on black). Empty spots accept Kings only.
Click a card to select → click destination. Double-click to auto-send to foundation.
Stock: Click the deck to draw. Undo available if you mess up.
