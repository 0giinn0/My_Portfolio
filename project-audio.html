What It Does

aud.io is a Flutter music player that searches YouTube Music, SoundCloud, and podcasts — all client-side. No backend. No server rack. No AWS bill at 3am. The app talks directly to the sources from your device. On web, requests route through free CORS proxies with automatic fallback. GitHub Pages hosts the web build. GitHub Releases hosts the APK and iOS app. That's the whole stack.

"But what about Spotify integration?" — It's there. OAuth login, fetch your playlists, one tap to import. We'll hold your library while you ditch the subscription. You're welcome.

Features

🔍 Search Everything

YouTube Music via InnerTube + SoundCloud, queried in parallel. Type a thing, get results from both. Like having two search engines in a trench coat.

🎙️ Podcasts

Podcast Index API powering search, trending, and episode playback. Free, open, no BS. Because someone had to make podcasts accessible without a subscription.

📁 Local Files

Scans your device for audio files. VLC-style. Finds your music so you don't have to dig through folders like some kind of digital archaeologist.

🎵 Golden Spiral Navigation

All 6 sections visible at once, arranged using the golden ratio (φ ≈ 0.618). Bauhaus-style numbered tiles. It's math you can tap. Because normal nav bars are boring.

📦 Bento Box UI

Asymmetric mixed-size cards with gradients, rounded corners. Every box is a different size — no uniform grids here. We don't do boring.

📥 Downloads

Direct MP3 downloads resolved client-side. Your music, your files, your problem now. No cloud required. No account. No "we lost your data" emails.

Themes

Ink & Red, Black & Grey, Black & Gold, Midnight Blue, Cream & Red, Pure White, Warm Sand. Pick one. Or switch every 5 minutes. We don't judge. Each theme completely transforms the app — from dark and moody to light and clean. It's like having 7 apps in one, except you only downloaded one. How economical.
