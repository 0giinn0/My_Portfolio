Each food = 10 points. The longer you get, the harder it gets. Story of my life.
Hit a wall or yourself? Game over. Start again. You'll get it eventually. Probably.

Blackjack
How to Play (21, not 22)
Goal: Beat the dealer without going over 21. Aces = 1 or 11. Face cards = 10. Only ♣ Clubs — 13 unique cards, no repeats (RE7 style). Deal → Hit → Stand. Dealer hits until 17+.

Solitaire
How to Play (patience pays off)
Goal: Move all cards to the 4 foundations (top right), A→K by suit. Tableau: 7 columns, build descending alternating colors. Empty spots = Kings only. Click card to select → click destination. Double-click = auto to foundation.
2048
How to Play (math just got interesting)
Swipe or use the arrow buttons to slide all tiles in a direction. Same numbers merge into one.
2+2=4. 4+4=8. You get it. Keep merging until you hit 2048.
Board fills up? Game over. The tiles are like pigeons — once they settle, they don't move.
